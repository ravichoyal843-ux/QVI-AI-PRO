Tripsavr Members - Getting Started
What is the validity period of my Tripsavr account?

Tripsavr 2.0 accounts are valid for 5 years from the date of purchase.

How can I log in to my Tripsavr account?

As a member, you need to fill in your Member ID and password on the following login page to access your account.

I did not receive my login credentials. What should I do?

Kindly check the inbox of your registered email address for the Welcome Letter. If you do not find it, please check your Spam/Junk mail folder as it might have been redirected there. If you are still having trouble accessing your account, please contact our Customer Care at help@myqvi.com.

How do I change or reset my password?

You can reset it at any time by using the “Forgot Password” option on the login page of the website.

What happens if my Tripsavr 2.0 account expires?

Your Tripsavr account will automatically be deactivated when your Tripsavr package expires. Once your account is deactivated, any balance of Travel Savings and/or Travel Credits in your account will be forfeited.

How to renew my Tripsavr 2.0 account once it has expired?

Your Tripsavr account will automatically be renewed every time you purchase a Tripsavr package. The expiry date of your Tripsavr 2.0 account will be 5 years from your last date of purchase.
