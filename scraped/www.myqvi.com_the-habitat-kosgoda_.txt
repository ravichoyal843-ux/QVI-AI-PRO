About This Hotel

The Habitat, Turtle Beach, Kosgoda by Asia Leisure, is 95 km away from the capital city of Colombo. An hour and 52 minute drive on the Southern Expressway through lush shades of tropical green to golden sand and sapphire blue ocean.

Let our relaxed homey comfort and true islander spirit seep in. Sounds of waves, feisty beach vibes, aroma of spicy cuisine and taste of local delicacies, your passage to days of sunny smiles and nights of tranquil contentment.

Our sole purpose is ensuring we live up to our name, keeping you basking in the joys of warm and sincere Sri Lankan hospitality in your tropical home, The Habitat.
