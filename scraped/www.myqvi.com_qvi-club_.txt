Club Applicable Fees

Know the fees that may apply to your membership.

Club Classic

 

Annual Fee	A fee payable to keep your membership active.	USD 40
Usage Fee	A fee payable to activate one week of entitlement.	USD 200
Club Booking Fee
	A fee applicable when you book from the Worldwide Selection Properties.	From USD 118
Split Week Club Booking Fee	A fee is payable per booking when you split your week entitlement into two (2) separate bookings at Worldwide Selection properties.	From USD 78
Cancellation Fee	A fee that is charged on confirmed bookings at Home Resorts & Seasonal Collection properties if cancellations are made within 30 days of check-in.	USD 50
Guest Certificate Fee	A fee charged if a member chooses to gift his entitlement to another person. This fee is waived for a member’s parents, spouse, and children.	USD 50
Membership Card
Replacement Fee	A fee charged to replace the membership card if the original is misplaced by the member.	USD 50
Transfer of Ownership Fee	A fee applicable if a member wishes to transfer his membership to another individual.	USD 150
Extension Fee (6 Months)	A fee required if a member wishes to extend his weeks entitlement for 6 months.
(Only for weeks entitlement with paid Usage Fee).	USD 30
Extension Fee (12 Months)	A fee required if a member wishes to extend his weeks entitlement for 12 months.
(Only for weeks entitlement with paid Usage Fee).	USD 60
