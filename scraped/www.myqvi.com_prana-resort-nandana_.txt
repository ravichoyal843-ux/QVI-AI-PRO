About This Hotel

Our top pick in Koh Samui! - 5 mins from Samui Airport.
Relax your mind, invigorate your body and revitalise your soul at Prana Resort Nandana. A charming haven where Thai tradition meets modernity, it is located on Bangrak Beach in the tranquil northern coast of Koh Samui. Whether travelling for leisure or business, the soothing atmosphere and signature Amezcua spa treatment on offer is sure to refresh and inspire creativity. Now renovated and reopening in May of this year, your comfort has just been upgraded.
