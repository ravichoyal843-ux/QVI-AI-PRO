General Breaks FAQs
What are QVI Home Resorts?

Home resorts are properties either fully or partially owned or invested in by QVI. We are happy to welcome you at:

Prana Resort Nandana – Koh Samui, Thailand.
BYD Lofts Boutique – Phuket, Thailand.
Doğan Hotel – Antalya, Turkey.
One @Bukit Ceylon – Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia.

What is a Split Week?

Split Week allows you to break a week of entitlement into two (2) stays – three (3) nights or four (4) nights.

What is a Guest Certificate?

Guest Certificate allows you to share your QVI Breaks week with your friends or family. However, the Guest Certificate Fee is waived for the following category of travellers: parents, spouse, and your kids, upon submission of supporting documents.

What is a Premium Booking Fee?

A Premium Booking Fee is a fee that may be required in particular cases such as (but not limited to) booking requests for specific travel dates/destinations, peak season travel periods, room/unit upgrade requests, etc.

Further information on various destinations and the details about pricing and fees that suit your preferences can be found on the Destinations page.
