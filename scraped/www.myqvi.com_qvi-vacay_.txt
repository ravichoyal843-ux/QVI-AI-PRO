How to Book with QVI Vacay

Log in and Search

Log in to your QVI Vacay account and enter your travel details to start searching for hotels.

Select and Book

Choose a hotel by clicking “Details”, view the details, select the room type, and the number of rooms to book.

Review and Pay

Review your booking details, fill in guest information, and select your preferred payment method.

Confirm and Enjoy

After payment confirmation, you will receive a confirmation voucher via email. Start creating memories!

Vacay Terms

Know the terms we use for all things Vacay.

Vacay Packages

Vacay Nights	These are flat rates enjoyed on selected nights at amazing hotels worldwide.
Vacay Deals	These are Special rates and VIP discounts on selected accommodations worldwide. In addition, the coupons offered are used to offset a certain amount of the booking fee.
Vacay Rewards	Year-end rewards for active Vacay Members with personal and/or referral’s consumed bookings.
Vacay Points	The accumulated points earned from consumed bookings made by a Vacay Member and his/her referral. 1 Point is equivalent to USD 1 spent on the consumed booking.
Vacay VIP Tier
(3,4,5,6,7)	A tiered system where users unlock higher discounts by booking and consuming more Vacay bookings. The Vacay VIP Tier ranges from 3% – 7%.
Vacay Wallet	Vacay Wallet is one of Vacay’s payment options and where Vacay Credits are stored.
Vacay Credits	Vacay Credits are amounts valued in USD and can be used to pay for bookings.
Vacay Member	A Vacay Member is an individual who has purchased QVI Vacay products.
Referral	A referral is a retail customer who has been referred by a Vacay Member.
Coupon	A virtual voucher that enables Vacay Members to offset a certain amount from the booking fee of Vacay Deals.
Vacay Eaze	Credits that can only be redeemed by logging in to your Virtual Office and can be used to pay for bookings made on Vacay.
