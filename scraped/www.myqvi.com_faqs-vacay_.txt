General FAQs about Vacay
How can I become a Vacay User?

To become a Vacay User, just simply purchase any Vacay product we offer.

What is the next step after I purchase QVI Vacay?

You will receive an e-Welcome letter in your registered email address with your login credentials to the QVI Vacay website. The credentials will include your IR ID and a temporary password. You will also receive an e-Certificate which will provide you with the details of your purchase. The e-Certificate is also stored in your Virtual Office (VO) for future reference or retrieval.

What is the difference between Vacay Basic and Vacay Regular?

With Vacay Basic, your Vacay Nights can only be used in any QVI Home Resorts worldwide. However, with Vacay Regular, your Vacay Nights can be also used in other destinations than QVI Home Resorts.
