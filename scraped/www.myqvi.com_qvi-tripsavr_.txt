What is Travel Savings?

When you purchase a QVI Tripsavr 2.0 package, you will receive your Travel Savings.
With Travel Savings, you can enjoy exclusive discounts on your bookings.
Travel Savings are automatically assigned by the system to your QVI Tripsavr 2.0 account.
What is a Travel Credits?

Each time you make a booking, you will be rewarded with Travel Credits. In addition, you will also earn whenever your Preferred Customers use their Travel Savings.
Travel Credits can be used for future bookings or withdrawn when you reach $250 or more.
What is a Preferred Customer?

Anyone you refer to use QVI Tripsavr 2.0 will become your Preferred Customer.
A Preferred Customer account is ONLY valid for 6 months.

How to Book with QVI Tripsavr

Log in and Search

Log in to your QVI Tripsavr 2.0 account and enter your travel details to start your hotel search.

Select and Book

Choose a hotel by clicking “Select Room”, view the details, and book the room type that suits you best.

Review and Pay

Fill in your travel or guest details and select your preferred payment method.

Confirm and Enjoy

After payment confirmation, receive your hotel voucher via email within minutes. Your next vacation is set!
