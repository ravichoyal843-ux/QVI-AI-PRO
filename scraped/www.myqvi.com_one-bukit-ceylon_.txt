About This Hotel

One@Bukit Ceylon is a upscale hotel suite right in the heart of KL city centre, yet enclaved in its own leafy neighbourhood. Enjoy the tranquil and serenity of the premier lifestyle area, while being minutes away from world class shopping malls, exceptional street food, an international dining scene, world famous tourist attractions and cultural heritage. One@Bukit Ceylon has it all.

Every studio suite is tastefully furnished and every effort has been made to infuse comfort and sophistication. For more space or to accommodate an extra guest, opt for the Studio Executive suite, which can fit an extra bed upon request.

The room is cleverly designed to house a washing machine cum dryer, a fridge, and electric hob and hood. There is even a toaster for morning breakfast or supper needs. Perfect for medium and long stay vacations where convenience is a factor. Cutlery, pots and plates are all thoughtfully provided.
